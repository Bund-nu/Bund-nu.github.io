General course objectives
The primary focus of this course is to elevate the students to take a triple bong, consisting of three green Tuborg.
Learning objectives
A student who has met the objectives of the course will be able to:
  • Take a triple bong.
Content
The course consists of lectures and demonstrations by Gustav Leth-Espensen & Matteo Christian Vogt. This is followed by a workshop in which the students get an oppertunity to try the techniques for themselves.
